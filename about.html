Why Is Filling the Right College Preference List So Important?

The college choice filling process can make or break your future. Whether you are applying through JEE Main, JEE Advanced, MHT-CET, or NEET-UG, the order of your college preferences determines which college and course you finally get.

Here's why filling your preference list correctly is crucial:

  • Avoid being over-ambitious: Listing only top colleges above your rank may leave you without any seat.
  • Avoid being too safe: Choosing only lower-ranked options can waste your potential and career prospects.
  • Strategic balance between ambitious, realistic, and safe options ensures the best outcomes.

NextStep ensures that your college list is perfectly balanced to maximize your chances of getting into the best possible college based on your score.

Common Mistakes Students Make During College Counselling

  • Not including backup colleges, resulting in no admission.
  • Confusing college names and codes (especially in MHT-CET choice filling).
  • Ignoring reservation policies and eligibility requirements.
  • Choosing colleges without considering future goals like placements, research, or higher studies.

At NextStep, we guide you to avoid these mistakes and make well-informed decisions.
