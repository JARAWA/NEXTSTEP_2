Frequently Asked Questions

What is NextStep?

NextStep is an intelligent college choice filling platform designed to help students maximize their entrance exam results. We analyze your rank, category, and preferences to create an optimized college preference list for JOSAA, MHT-CET, and NEET-UG counselling processes.

How does NextStep generate my college list?

Our algorithm processes multiple factors including:

  • Your entrance exam rank
  • Category and reservation status
  • Home state for quota eligibility
  • Previous years' opening and closing ranks
  • Latest admission trends and patterns

This creates a personalized list with strategic placement of ambitious, realistic, and safe options to maximize your chances of securing the best possible seat.

Which counselling processes does NextStep support?

We currently provide comprehensive support for:

  • JEE Main - JOSAA counselling for NITs, IIITs, and GFTIs
  • JEE Advanced - IIT admissions
  • MHT-CET - Engineering and Pharmacy admissions in Maharashtra
  • NEET-UG - Medical admissions including MBBS, BDS, and AYUSH courses
Why is choice filling so critical?

Your college preference list is the single most important factor that determines your final admission outcome, often even more important than small differences in rank. A strategically filled preference list can:

  • Significantly improve your chances of admission to top colleges
  • Protect you from getting no seat allocation despite having a decent rank
  • Ensure you don't miss opportunities at better colleges due to unfamiliarity with the system
  • Maximize the value of your hard-earned entrance exam performance
Is NextStep free to use?

Yes! We currently offer our core college list generation service completely free of charge. We believe every student deserves access to strategic admission guidance regardless of their financial situation.

In the future, we may introduce premium features with advanced insights and personalized counselling, but our basic service will remain free.

Can I edit my generated preference list?

Absolutely! NextStep provides you with a data-driven starting point, but we understand personal preferences matter too. You can freely modify your preference list by:

  • Reordering colleges based on location preferences
  • Prioritizing specific branches or courses you're passionate about
  • Adding or removing institutions based on your research
  • Fine-tuning your strategy before finalizing it for official submission
