JOSAA 2025 Overview

The Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JOSAA) conducts the counselling process for admission to 23 IITs, 31 NITs, IIEST Shibpur, 26 IIITs, and 47 Other Government Funded Technical Institutes (GFTIs) for the academic year 2025-26.

Official Schedule: JOSAA 2025 registration begins on June 3, 2025 at 5:00 PM and continues until June 12, 2025 at 5:00 PM. The process includes 6 rounds of seat allocation with the final round for IITs ending on July 21, 2025, followed by special CSAB rounds for NIT+ system vacant seats.

New Feature: Starting from Round 2, candidates can initiate withdrawal from the seat allocation process. Pre-payment of seat acceptance fee is also available during the registration period.

Eligibility Criteria

For IIT Admissions

  • JEE Advanced 2025 Qualification: Must be among the qualified candidates in JEE Advanced 2025
  • Class 12 Performance: Minimum 75% marks in Class 12 (or equivalent) for General/EWS candidates, 65% for SC/ST/PwD candidates
  • Year of Passing: Must have appeared for Class 12 for the first time in 2024 or 2025
  • Subject Requirements: Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics must be among the subjects in Class 12
  • Age Limit: No specific age limit

For NIT/IIIT/GFTI Admissions

  • JEE Main 2025 Qualification: Must have a valid JEE Main 2025 rank
  • Class 12 Performance: Minimum 75% marks in Class 12 for General/EWS, 65% for SC/ST/PwD
  • Subject Requirements: Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in Class 12
  • Year of Passing: 2023, 2024, or 2025 (maximum two attempts allowed)

Special Note for Architecture Courses: Candidates seeking admission to B.Arch courses must also qualify the Architecture Aptitude Test (AAT) conducted after JEE Main.

JOSAA 2025 Counselling Process

1
Registration

Visit josaa.nic.in and register using your JEE Main/Advanced application number, password, and personal details. Complete OTP verification and create your JOSAA portal password.

2
Choice Filling

Fill your preferred choices of institutes and programs in order of preference. You can add, modify, and rearrange choices multiple times before the deadline.

3
Choice Locking

Lock your final choices by June 12, 2025. After locking, no changes will be permitted. If not locked manually, choices will be auto-locked at the deadline.

4
Mock Allocation

Check mock allocation results on June 9 and June 11 to understand your chances. Modify choices if needed before the final deadline.

5
Seat Allocation

Check seat allocation results for each round. If allocated a seat, choose from FREEZE, FLOAT, or SLIDE options for subsequent rounds.

6
Fee Payment & Reporting

Pay the seat acceptance fee and upload required documents. Report to the allocated institute for final admission and fee payment.

Important Distinctions: IIT vs NIT+ System

IIT Timeline

Rounds 1-6: All 6 rounds of counselling
Final Round: Round 6 (July 16, 2025)
Process Ends: July 21, 2025 after fee payment

NIT+ System Timeline

Rounds 1-6: All 6 rounds of counselling
Additional Process: Withdrawal option in Round 6
PAF Payment: July 23-27, 2025
CSAB Rounds: Late July 2025

Key Point: Round 6 is the FINAL ROUND for IIT admissions, but NIT+ system has additional processes including Partial Admission Fee (PAF) payment and CSAB special rounds for any remaining vacant seats.

Seat Allocation Options

FREEZE

Accept the allocated seat and do not participate in further rounds. This decision is final and cannot be changed.

FLOAT

Accept the current seat but remain open for a better institute or branch in higher preference in subsequent rounds.

SLIDE

Accept the current seat but remain open for a better branch in the same institute in subsequent rounds.

Important: SLIDE and FLOAT options are NOT available in the final round (Round 6). In the final round, you can only FREEZE your seat.

Important Tips & Guidelines

New Features in JOSAA 2025

  • Pre-payment facility: You can pre-pay the seat acceptance fee during registration (June 3-12)
  • Withdrawal option: Starting from Round 2, you can withdraw from the allocation process if needed
  • AAT integration: Architecture candidates can fill AAT-specific choices from June 8, 2025
  • Extended deadlines: Fee payment resolution periods are provided for technical issues

Choice Filling Strategy

  • Fill maximum choices: There's no limit on the number of choices you can fill
  • Order matters: Arrange choices in strict order of your preference
  • Include safe options: Don't fill only dream colleges; include realistic and safe options
  • Consider all factors: Location, branch preference, college ranking, and future prospects
  • Use mock allocations: Analyze mock results to fine-tune your choices

During Counselling

  • Check your allotment status regularly on the portal
  • Respond to document verification queries promptly
  • Pay fees within the specified deadlines
  • Keep all original documents ready for reporting
  • Don't wait until the last moment for any action

Remember: JOSAA follows a transparent, merit-based allocation system. The algorithm considers your rank, choices, and seat availability to make allocations. Stay patient and trust the process.
